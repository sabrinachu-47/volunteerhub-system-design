# VolunteerHub – Volunteer Management Platform Design

This project proposes **VolunteerHub**, a centralized platform designed to connect volunteers with organizations and streamline volunteer recruitment, scheduling, and management.

The system was developed as part of a **Systems Analysis and Design project**, focusing on the planning and architecture of a scalable volunteer management platform.

---

## Problem

Many communities lack a centralized platform for connecting volunteers with organizations.  
Existing solutions are fragmented, making it difficult for:

- volunteers to find opportunities
- organizations to recruit and manage volunteers

VolunteerHub aims to solve this by providing a unified platform with intelligent matching and management tools.

---

## Key Features

- Volunteer opportunity search & filtering
- One-click volunteer applications
- Organization dashboards for applicant management
- Messaging system for volunteers and organizations
- Personalized opportunity recommendations
- Verified volunteer hour tracking
- Rating and review system
